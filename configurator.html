What is a Congruent Box?

  • Boxes that are exactly identical in Length, Width, Height, and Shape
  • Manufactured to precise dimensional tolerances (±5 mm)
  • Every box in a batch matches perfectly

Benefits of Congruent Boxes

  • Perfect stacking on pallets
  • Efficient warehouse storage
  • Uniform palletization for shipping
  • Reduced shipping damage
  • Accurate load and cost calculation
  • Professional packaging standard
