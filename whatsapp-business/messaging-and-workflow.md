## Labels

Create these labels exactly:

- `New Lead`
- `Hot Quote`
- `Bulk Order`
- `Custom Size`
- `Printed Box`
- `Follow Up`
- `Quoted`
- `Order Confirmed`

## Manual Lead Workflow

1. Apply `New Lead` to every new inquiry.
2. Send the greeting message or `/quote`.
3. Apply `Hot Quote` or `Bulk Order` when inquiry size is serious.
4. Apply `Custom Size` when dimensions are non-standard.
5. Apply `Printed Box` when the buyer asks for branding or custom print.
6. After quotation is shared, change the lead label to `Quoted`.
7. After payment or final confirmation, change the lead label to `Order Confirmed`.
8. If waiting for customer response, apply `Follow Up`.

## Follow-Up Timing

- First follow-up: `24 hours`
- Second follow-up: `3 days`
- Stop repeated follow-ups after `7 days` unless the customer re-engages

## WhatsApp Web Operating Rules

- Reply from Web during business hours.
- Turn on browser notifications.
- Use one dedicated Chrome profile for business handling.
- Keep product images ready to send from desktop.
- Pin active customer chats.
